### Key Concepts

* **Systems Engineering**: The process of designing, developing, and operating complex systems, such as vehicles. It considers the entire system, including hardware and software, to ensure that it meets its intended function.
* **ISO 26262**: A functional safety standard for automotive systems, which views functional safety as a systems engineering problem rather than just a hardware or software issue.
* **V Diagram**: A tool used in systems engineering to visualize the development process. It places hardware and software development at the bottom of the diagram, emphasizing that they are part of the larger system.
