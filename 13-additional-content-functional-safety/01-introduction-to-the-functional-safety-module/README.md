## Summary

**Functional Safety in Automotive Systems**
=============================================

This module focuses on analyzing and designing automotive systems to reduce risk. It introduces the framework provided by ISO 26262, an international standard for functional safety.

**Key Concepts**
-----------------

* **Functional Safety**: A critical aspect of designing autonomous vehicles that ensures the system's safe operation.
* **ISO 26262**: An international standard for functional safety in automotive systems.
* **Risk Reduction**: Analyzing and designing systems to minimize potential hazards and ensure safe operation.
* **Validation and Verification**: Ensuring products meet requirements through testing before production.

**Practical Notes**
-------------------

This module does not include any specific practical steps or code patterns. However, it provides an overview of the importance of functional safety in automotive systems and introduces the framework provided by ISO 26262. The instructors, Dheeraj, Stephanie, and Benjamin, are experts in functional safety and will guide you through the techniques used at Elektrobit to ensure safe and reliable products.
