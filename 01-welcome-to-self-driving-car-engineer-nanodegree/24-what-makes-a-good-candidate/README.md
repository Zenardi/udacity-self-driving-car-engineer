## Summary

**Summary**

This lesson discusses the importance of having expertise in multiple areas, specifically research and engineering skills, to be successful in a career at Waymo or similar companies. The instructor shares personal experience leading a research team and highlights the value of integrating research and engineering skills.

**Key Concepts**

* **Research-Engineering Cycle**: A cycle where research leads to development of promising ideas, which are then integrated into products through engineering, resulting in new ideas and further innovation.
* **Interdisciplinary Skills**: Having expertise in both research and engineering is crucial for a successful career in the industry, making individuals more productive members of their company and community.
* **Continuous Learning**: Staying up-to-date with the latest developments in one's field is essential for engineers to remain relevant and valuable.

**Practical Notes**

* To become a well-rounded professional, focus on developing both research and engineering skills.
* Consider working on projects that integrate research and engineering to gain practical experience.
* Stay current with industry developments by attending conferences, reading research papers, or participating in online forums.
