## Summary

**Autonomous Driving Technology: Testing and Validation**
===========================================================

This summary covers the key concepts and practical notes from a lesson on testing and validation of autonomous driving technology.

### Key Concepts
* **Autonomous driving technology**: A system that enables vehicles to navigate roads without human intervention, using sensors, software, and machine learning algorithms.
* **Waymo driver**: The name given to Waymo's autonomous driving technology, which has been tested in real-world scenarios and simulated environments.
* **Simulation testing**: A method of validating the behavior of autonomous driving technology before it is deployed on public roads, where tens of billions of miles have been autonomously driven.

### Practical Notes
* Testing autonomous driving technology involves a combination of real-world testing (over 20 million miles) and simulation testing (tens of billions of miles).
* Specialized facilities, such as golf courses, can be designed for unique testing needs.
* The Waymo driver has been tested in various environments, including public roads and specially designed testing facilities.
