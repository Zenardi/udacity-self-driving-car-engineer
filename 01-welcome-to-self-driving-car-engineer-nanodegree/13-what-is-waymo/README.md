## Summary

**Autonomous Driving Technology with Waymo**
==========================================

Waymo is a company that specializes in developing autonomous driving technology to make transportation safer and more convenient. With over a decade of experience, Waymo has made significant progress in the field.

### Key Concepts
* **Autonomous driving**: vehicles that can operate without human intervention
* **Simulation testing**: virtual environment for testing autonomous vehicle behavior
* **Commercial deployment**: use of autonomous technology in real-world applications (e.g. ride-hailing services)
* **Edge cases**: rare or unusual scenarios that require special consideration in autonomous vehicle development

### Practical Notes
Waymo has successfully deployed a commercial, fully autonomous ride-hailing service called Waymo One in Chandler, Arizona. The service allows users to download an app and hail a self-driving vehicle for transportation. This achievement marks a significant milestone in the development of autonomous driving technology.

## Transcript

Waymo is an autonomous driving technology company with a mission to make it safe and easy to get people and things where they're going. We were founded in 2009 as the Google self-driving car project. Since then, we have autonomously driven tens of millions of miles on the railroads and tens of billions of miles in simulation. We're the first company to have a commercial 100 percent fully autonomous ride-hailing service on public roads, Waymo One, anyone in our service territory, which is currently in Chandler, Arizona, can download our app and hail a fully autonomous Waymo vehicle and experience the truly driverless experience. We also see other use cases for the driver, including autonomous trucking, and delivery, and eventually personal car ownership.
