## Summary
The "How to Succeed" video provides strategies for effectively learning new technical skills in a self-paced online program like Udacity. The presenters emphasize the importance of setting clear goals, both short-term and long-term, to guide your learning journey. They suggest creating an incremental plan to achieve these goals and holding yourself accountable through reminders or by sharing your goals with others.

The video encourages students to break down projects into smaller tasks, work on exercises to reinforce learning, and utilize resources when facing challenges. It highlights the value of patience and persistence, reminding students that making mistakes is part of the learning process. The presenters also stress the importance of community resources, such as forums and online searches, to help troubleshoot issues.

Overall, the video aims to motivate students to stay committed, develop good habits, and approach their learning with a positive mindset.
