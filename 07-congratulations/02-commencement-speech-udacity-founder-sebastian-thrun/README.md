## Summary

**Graduation Commencement Speech: Celebrating Your Achievement and Inspiring Future Success**

**Key Concepts**

* **DARPA Grand Challenge**: A robot racing competition where teams had to navigate a 131-mile course in the Mojave Desert, highlighting the importance of perseverance and believing in oneself.
* **Self-driving cars**: The concept of autonomous vehicles that can safely navigate public roads, which was initially considered impossible but became a reality through determination and innovation.
* **Believing in oneself**: A key takeaway from Sebastian's stories, emphasizing the importance of trusting one's abilities and not giving up on seemingly impossible goals.

**Practical Notes**

While this lesson is more inspirational than technical, it highlights the value of:

* **Perseverance**: Don't give up on your goals, even when faced with seemingly insurmountable challenges.
* **Collaboration**: Work with others to achieve something greater than you could alone.
* **Innovation**: Be willing to challenge conventional wisdom and explore new ideas.

This lesson encourages graduates to remember their accomplishments, energy, determination, sense of community, family, and friends. It inspires them to believe in themselves, dare to dream impossible dreams, and strive to make the world a better place.
