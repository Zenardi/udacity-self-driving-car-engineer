## Extracurricular Content

While you may have checked some of it out during the Nanodegree itself, there is also additional "Extracurricular" content available on different self-driving car topics, which you can find after exiting the lesson, under the "Extracurricular" heading on the left side of the classroom (you may have to scroll down past all of the "Core Curriculum", or collapse that header).


With the exception of the Autonomous Systems Interview section, each of these additional content areas do not include graded projects of their own, so they are outside of the core content of the Nanodegree program. While they are completely optional, if you have a strong interest in any of these areas (or are just looking for even more self-driving car content!), you should check them out.

- Kalman Filters
  - Unscented Kalman Filters
- Prediction
- Control
  - Vehicle Models
  - Model Predictive Control
- Deep Learning
  - Fully Convolutional Networks
  - Scene Understanding
  - Inference Performance
- Functional Safety
  - Safety Plan
  - Hazard Analysis and Risk Assessment
  - Functional Safety Concept
  - Technical Safety Concept
  - Functional Safety at the Software and Hardware Levels
- Autonomous Systems Interview - prep for an interview for an autonomous systems role!
