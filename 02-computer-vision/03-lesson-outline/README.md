## Summary

**Course Overview**
================

This introductory lesson sets the stage for the entire course by outlining its content and scope. The main focus is on self-driving car systems, where **deep learning** plays a crucial role.

**Key Concepts**
---------------

* **Deep Learning**: A high-level concept that will be explored in detail throughout the course.
* **Computer Vision**: A key aspect of self-driving cars that enables them to perceive and understand their environment.
* **Stakeholders**: The individuals or groups who will be impacted by the development and deployment of self-driving car technology, including users, manufacturers, regulators, and more.

**Practical Notes**
-----------------

* Familiarity with coding is assumed, as students will need to implement code throughout the course.
* Review of necessary **tools**, such as programming languages and software libraries, will be provided.
* The final project will serve as a culmination of the skills learned throughout the course.
