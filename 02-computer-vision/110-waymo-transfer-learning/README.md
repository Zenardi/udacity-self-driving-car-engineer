## Summary

**Transfer Learning: Applying Domain Knowledge Across Tasks**
===========================================================

Transfer learning is a powerful technique in machine learning where knowledge gained from one specific task or domain can be applied to another related but distinct task or domain. This approach enables models to adapt quickly to new environments and improve their performance without requiring extensive retraining.

### Key Concepts
* **Domain**: A specific area of expertise, such as image classification or natural language processing.
* **Task**: A particular problem or objective within a domain, like object detection in images.
* **Transfer Learning**: The ability to leverage knowledge from one task or domain and apply it to another related but different task or domain.

### Practical Notes
When applying transfer learning, consider the following:
* Train models on a diverse dataset that captures various aspects of the original domain.
* Use pre-trained models as a starting point for your own model development.
* Evaluate the performance of your model in the target domain and adjust accordingly.
