## Summary

**Autonomous Systems Interview Course Overview**
=====================================================

This course provides guidance and support for individuals preparing for interviews in autonomous systems, including self-driving cars, robotics, and flying cars. The course is led by Michael, a graduate of Udacity's Self-Driving Car Engineer program.

**Key Concepts**
---------------

* **Autonomous systems**: A broad field that encompasses self-driving cars, robotics, and other technologies that enable machines to operate independently.
* **Thought process**: The interviewer wants to see the candidate's thought process behind tackling a problem, not just their ability to solve it quickly.
* **Problem-solving approach**: Candidates should demonstrate a clear and logical approach to solving problems, including identifying key issues and developing solutions.

**Practical Notes**
-----------------

* This course includes example interviews where candidates are presented with problems and asked to respond as they would in a real interview.
* After each video, students will have the opportunity to reflect on what they thought of the candidate's performance, including their strengths and weaknesses.
* The interviewer's feedback is also provided, highlighting what they liked about the candidate's approach and what they could improve on.
