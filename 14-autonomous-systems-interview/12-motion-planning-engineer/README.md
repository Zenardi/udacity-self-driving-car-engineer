**Key Concepts**

* **Finite State Machines**: A mathematical model used to describe the behavior of a system by defining its states and transitions between them.
* **Model Predictive Controllers**: An algorithmic approach to control systems that predicts future behavior based on current state and optimizes control inputs to achieve desired outcomes.
* **Motion Planning**: The process of determining the optimal path for an autonomous vehicle to follow, taking into account various constraints such as speed limits, lane changes, and obstacles.
* **Cost Functions**: Mathematical functions used to evaluate the desirability of a particular motion plan, often based on metrics such as safety, efficiency, or comfort.
