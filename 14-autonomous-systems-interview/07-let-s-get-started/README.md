## Summary

**Watching Interviews for Autonomous Systems Roles**
=====================================================

This lesson guides you through the process of watching and evaluating candidate performance during technical interviews for autonomous systems roles. You will learn how to effectively assess a candidate's problem-solving skills, approach, and overall fit for the position.

### Key Concepts

* **Candidate Evaluation**: Take notes on each candidate's approach to the problem, highlighting what they do well and areas for improvement.
* **Reflective Thinking**: Put yourself in the interviewer's shoes to evaluate the candidate's performance. Ask yourself if you would trust them to perform the job responsibilities.
* **Interviewer Feedback**: Compare your own thoughts with the interviewer's feedback after each video.

### Practical Notes

When watching the interviews, follow these steps:

1. Take detailed notes on each candidate's approach and problem-solving skills.
2. Reflect on their performance as if you were the hiring manager or a teammate.
3. Evaluate whether they demonstrate the necessary capabilities for the role.
4. Compare your thoughts with the interviewer's feedback after each video.

Remember, not every candidate will have a perfect response. The goal is to assess their potential and fit for the position.
