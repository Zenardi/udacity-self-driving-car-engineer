## Summary

**Autonomous System Skills and Job Roles**
=============================================

This summary covers the importance of autonomous system skills in job roles, particularly in the fields of motion planning, control systems, and perception engineering.

### Key Concepts
* **Autonomous Systems**: Refers to self-governing systems that can operate independently without human intervention.
* **Motion Planning Engineer**: Responsible for designing algorithms and strategies for a robot or vehicle to navigate through its environment.
* **Controls Engineer**: Focuses on developing control systems to regulate the behavior of autonomous systems, ensuring they meet performance and safety requirements.
* **Perception Engineer**: Concerned with enabling autonomous systems to perceive their surroundings, including tasks such as object detection, tracking, and scene understanding.

### Practical Notes
The lesson suggests that job roles in autonomous systems are often specialized, with specific titles like motion planning engineer or perception engineer. It's recommended to review each interview, even if the question is not directly related to your desired role, as it may provide valuable insights into problem-solving strategies and approaches.
