## Summary

**Preparing for Autonomous Systems Interviews**
=====================================================

This README file provides an overview of key concepts and practical notes related to preparing for autonomous systems interviews.

**Key Concepts**
---------------

* **Role-specific questions**: Questions tailored to specific roles, such as deep learning engineer or controls engineer.
* **Recent project discussion**: The importance of discussing a recent project you've worked on to demonstrate expertise and passion in the area.
* **Articulating project choices**: Being able to explain why you chose a particular project and what steps/missteps were taken along the way.
* **Project evaluation criteria**: Considering factors such as end result, interest, and potential for improvement when discussing a recent project.

**Practical Notes**
------------------

When preparing for autonomous systems interviews, it's essential to be prepared to answer role-specific questions. This includes choosing questions from areas of your interest and being able to articulate why you chose specific projects. Additionally, be prepared to discuss a recent project you've worked on, highlighting the steps taken, missteps encountered, and potential improvements.

When discussing a recent project:

* Explain why you chose this project
* Describe the steps taken along with any missteps
* Highlight what made the end result interesting
* Discuss potential ways to further improve the project

By being prepared to answer these types of questions, you'll be better equipped to showcase your expertise and passion in autonomous systems.
