## Summary

**Preparing for Autonomous Systems Interviews**

This README file summarizes key concepts and practical steps to help you prepare for autonomous systems interviews. The goal is to provide a comprehensive overview of what to expect and how to approach these types of questions.

### Key Concepts

* **Autonomous Systems Roles**: Typically require proficiency in C++ programming language.
* **Data Structures and Algorithms**: Understanding runtime and complexity analysis is crucial for autonomous systems development.
* **Job Responsibilities and Requirements**: Refresh your knowledge on topics mentioned in job descriptions, minimum requirements, or listed on your resume.
* **Company Research**: Don't forget to research the company itself to demonstrate your interest and fit.

### Practical Notes

* Practice solving typical programming problems in C++ to build up your skills.
* Use online resources to review data structures and algorithms, including runtime and complexity analysis.
* Make sure you feel comfortable with topics mentioned in job descriptions or listed on your resume, such as systems architect and real-time operating systems.
