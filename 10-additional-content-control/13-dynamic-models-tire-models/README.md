## Summary

**Tire Modeling**

This README file provides a summary of key concepts related to tire modeling, including the behavior of tires and the mathematical models used to describe their performance.

**Key Concepts**
* **Slip Angle**: The angle between the direction of travel and the direction of the force applied to the tire.
* **Lateral Force (F)**: A force that acts perpendicular to the direction of travel, caused by the slip angle.
* **Alpha**: The slip angle, used in calculations for lateral force.
* **Longitudinal and Lateral Forces**: Forces acting along and across the direction of travel, respectively.
* **Pacejka Tire Model (Magic Tire Formula)**: A widely-used mathematical model that describes tire behavior.

**Practical Notes**
* Understanding the relationship between slip angle and lateral force is crucial for optimizing vehicle performance in various driving situations.
* The Pacejka tire model is a fundamental concept in tire dynamics, used to calculate longitudinal and lateral forces.
